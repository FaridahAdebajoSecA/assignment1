Genres

Romance

Romance novels focus on the relationship and romantic love between two people and often have an emotionally satisfying and optimistic ending.

Action

Action genres are characterized by a resourceful hero struggling against incredible odds, which include life-threatening situations, a villain, or a pursuit which generally concludes in victory for the hero.

Fantasy

Fantasy novels often feature magical and supernatural elements that do not exist in the real world, including wizards, elves, and dragons.
